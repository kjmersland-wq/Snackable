Spaced Repetition

The algorithm knows
when you're about to forget

SnackAble Learn implements the SM-2 algorithm — the same one Anki uses, based on Piotr Woźniak's groundbreaking research from 1987. It calculates exactly when you're about to forget a concept and sends a review reminder at precisely the right moment — not too early, not too late.

The result: you spend up to 90% less time on review, but retain just as much — because you're studying smart, not more.

SM-2 algorithm: calculates your individual optimal review interval
Push notifications and email reminders at the right moment
Tracks exactly which flashcards you know and which you forget
Shows progress stats per module and per course
Export your review data to Anki for offline use

Export

You own the knowledge.
Take it anywhere.

Export course content to the tools you already use — one click, four formats.

🃏
Anki
Export all flashcards as an Anki deck (.apkg). Open directly in Anki for offline Spaced Repetition on mobile and desktop. Compatible with AnkiDroid and AnkiMobile.
