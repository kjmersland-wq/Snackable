About us

KM Tech Labs

KM Tech Labs is a technology company based in Kristiansand, Norway. We build tools that make knowledge more accessible and genuinely useful — by combining modern AI with proven learning science.

SnackAble Learn is our flagship product: a tool that automatically transforms passive content into structured micro-courses built on Active Recall and Spaced Repetition. We believe everyone deserves access to effective learning tools — not just those with the time and resources to build them by hand.
